Assignment 0: Prelude due January 24

Complete the Getting Started guide for Ivory, a Hadoop toolkit for web-scale information retrieval research. This tutorial will take you through setting up Hadoop on your local machine and running Hadoop on the virtual machine image. It'll also begin familiarizing you with github.

Note: This assignment is optional and will not graded. However, the content overlaps with assignment 1, so completing this will make assignment 1 much easier.
