Welcome to the Seattle City Guide. The largest city in the Pacific Northwest, Seattle lies between the Puget Sound and Lake Washington. Though it it known for a rainy climate, Seattle actually has a warm, mild temperate marine climate and averages only 38 inches of rain per year.

Despite their heavy coffee consumption, Seattleites are among the healthiest urban dwellers in the country, as well as the most literate. As a result, the city has a vibrant and diverse cultural community and numerous outdoor activities to enjoy.

The reviews for Seattle were written by graduate students and young professionals who call Seattle home. Try some of our search features below and plan a day in Seattle!
